4EXT: Betatext
==============

.. only:: html

    :Classification:
        we_betatext

    :Version:
        |release|

    :Language:
        en

    :Description:
        Betatext does allow users to comment any part of a text. The comments can be rated up and
        down. Users may request to be marked as “verified” to show that they represent
        officially an institution, use their real name or are especially trusted. Betatext is used
        for collaborating work; One can leave constructive criticism towards specific words and
        phrases in a text and what may be added or changed before the final version of the document
        is printed. This extension is available on github: https://github.com/wegewerk/betatext.git
